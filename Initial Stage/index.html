Welcome to the Password Manager

This is a simple and secure password manager that helps you store your passwords for different websites and services. It keeps your passwords safe and accessible, while making it easier to manage all your login credentials in one place.

Key Features

Secure

All your passwords are encrypted and stored securely, ensuring your data remains safe from unauthorized access.

Easy to Use

Simple user interface that allows you to easily save and manage your passwords with a few clicks.

Cross-Platform

Access your passwords on any device with internet access, ensuring you can always manage your credentials, anywhere.

How It Works

  1. Create a new account with your email and a master password.
  2. Log in to your account securely with your credentials.
  3. Save and manage your passwords for websites directly within the app.
